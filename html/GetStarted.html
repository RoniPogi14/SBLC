DOCUMENT REQUEST SYSTEM

Welcome to the SBLC Document Request System

Welcome to the system that allows SBLC employees to manage, track, and process internal document requests.
Follow these steps to get started and use the system efficiently.

Step 1: Log In

Log in using your provided credentials. If you don't have an account, please contact your system administrator.

Step 2: Submit a Document Request

Once logged in, choose the document type you need and fill in the required details to submit your request.

Step 3: Track Your Requests

After submitting your request, you can track its progress. You will be notified when your request is approved or denied.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: What if I forget my password?
    A: You can reset it via the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
  • Q: How can I request multiple documents?
    A: Each request needs to be submitted separately.
